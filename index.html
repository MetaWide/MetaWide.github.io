Download MetaMask Extension: Chrome Install Help Guide

RC
Riley_Chen8 Year Member
November 22, 2025

MetaMask is the most popular cryptocurrency wallet and browser extension that lets you interact with Ethereum and thousands of decentralized applications (dApps). Whether you're new to Web3 or an experienced user, installing the official MetaMask extension on Chrome (or the MetaMask mobile app on your phone) is the first step to managing your crypto assets safely. This up-to-date guide walks you through how to download and install MetaMask on Chrome and mobile devices in just a few minutes using only official sources.

Prerequisites

  • Google Chrome, Brave, Edge, or any Chromium-based browser (latest version recommended)
  • A stable internet connection
  • At least 100 MB of free disk space
  • For mobile: iPhone with iOS 15+ or Android device with Android 8.0+

Step-by-Step: Install MetaMask Extension on Chrome

  1. Open the official MetaMask website
    Go to https://metamask.io in your Chrome browser. Always start here to avoid fake or phishing sites.
  2. Click "Download"
    On the homepage, click the large blue "Download button. MetaMask will automatically detect you're using Chrome.
  3. Choose "Install MetaMask for Chrome"
    You'll be redirected to the Chrome Web Store page for the official MetaMask extension (verified publisher: "https://metamask.io").
  4. Click "Add to Chrome"
    A pop-up will appear. Click the blueAdd to Chromebutton.
  5. Confirm installation
    Another confirmation window will ask "Add 'MetaMask'?". ClickAdd extension.
  6. Wait for the download and installation
    The extension will download and install automatically (usually under 10 seconds). You'll see the MetaMask fox icon appear in the top-right toolbar.
  7. Pin the extension (recommended)
    Click the puzzle icon ‚Üí find MetaMask ‚Üí click the pin icon so it's always visible.
  8. Click the MetaMask fox icon and select "Create a Wallet" or "Import Wallet"
    Follow the on-screen instructions to set up a new wallet or recover an existing one using your seed phrase.

Congratulations! You have successfully installed MetaMask on Chrome.

How to Install MetaMask Mobile App (iOS & Android)

  1. Open theApp Store(iPhone) orGoogle Play Store(Android).
  2. Search for "MetaMask" (developer: ConsenSys).
  3. TapGetorInstall.
  4. Open the app and chooseCreate a New WalletorImport using Secret Recovery Phrase.
  5. Follow the setup wizard (same process as desktop).

Phishing or fake MetaMask pop-upNever enter your seed phrase on any website. Only trust metamask.io and the official Chrome Web Store.

Important Security Tips After Installation

  • Write down your 12-word Secret Recovery Phrase on paper and store it offline.
  • Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone.
  • Enable 2FA on associated email accounts.
  • Always double-check website URLs before connecting your wallet.
  • Use hardware wallets (Ledger/Trezor) with MetaMask for large amounts.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is MetaMask free to download?

Yes, the extension and mobile app are 100% free.

Can I use MetaMask on Firefox or Safari?

Yes. Visit metamask.io and select the correct browser.

Is it safe to download MetaMask in 2025?

Yes, as long as you download from the official site (metamask.io) or verified app stores.

What's the difference between MetaMask extension and mobile app?

The extension is best for desktop dApps; the mobile app includes a built-in browser and easier mobile signing.

Can I have MetaMask on multiple devices?

Yes. Import the same Secret Recovery Phrase on each device.

I lost my seed phrase. What now?

Unfortunately, if you don't have the seed phrase, your funds are permanently lost. MetaMask cannot recover them.
